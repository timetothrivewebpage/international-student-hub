Accessibility

Accessibility goals and limitations for this student research prototype.

Accessibility approach

This prototype aims to use clear headings, readable spacing, descriptive links, keyboard focus states, and responsive layouts that work on mobile and desktop screens.

If something is difficult to read, navigate, or understand, that feedback is useful for improving the prototype.

Known limitations

Some features are still prototypes, including site-wide search and feedback submission. Content should be checked against official source pages before decisions are made.

Student accommodations

For official University of Alberta accessibility and accommodations support, use the linked resources in the FAQ or official UAlberta pages.
