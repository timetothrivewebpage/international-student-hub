Privacy Notice

How feedback form information is intended to be used in this research prototype.

Prototype privacy notice

The feedback form is intended to help the research team understand whether this student hub is useful, clear, and easy to navigate. This notice is a plain-language project notice, not legal advice or a substitute for institutional research ethics guidance.

Information requested

The planned feedback form asks for name, University CCID, department or faculty, year of study, country of origin, status in Canada, and written feedback about the prototype.

Purpose

Responses should be used to improve the navigation, wording, content structure, and usability of the International Student Hub prototype.

Do not submit urgent details

The form is not monitored for urgent support. Do not include medical details, immigration case details, emergency requests, or highly sensitive personal information in the feedback box.

Before launch

Before collecting responses, the research team should connect the form to an approved survey or storage system, confirm who can access responses, decide how long responses are retained, and add any required consent or ethics language.
