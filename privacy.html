Privacy Notice

How feedback form information is intended to be used in this research prototype.

Prototype privacy notice

The feedback form helps the project team understand whether this student hub is useful, clear, and easy to navigate. This notice is a plain-language project notice, not legal advice or a substitute for institutional research ethics guidance.

Information requested

The form asks for year of study, status in Canada, and written feedback. Name, University CCID, department or faculty, and country of origin are optional.

Purpose

Responses are used to improve navigation, wording, content structure, and usability. Optional CCID information is used only to understand whether feedback is from a UAlberta community member.

Do not submit urgent details

The form is not monitored for urgent support. Do not include medical details, immigration case details, emergency requests, student ID numbers, or highly sensitive personal information.

How submissions are handled

The form is prepared to use Formspree as a third-party form processing service. When enabled, Formspree receives the submission, sends an email notification to the project team, and stores submission history according to the active Formspree plan. The free plan currently stores submission history for 30 days.

Access and retention

Feedback should be reviewed only by the project team for prototype improvement. The team should export or delete submissions according to the project’s approved retention plan, and should avoid keeping identifying information longer than needed.
